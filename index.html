Features

Everything you need
to own your finances

Built specifically for students juggling tuition, rent, food, and everything in between.

Expense Tracking

Log transactions instantly. Tag categories, add notes, and see exactly where your money goes every day.

Smart Budgets

Set monthly limits per category. Get notified when you're close. Stay disciplined without the stress.

Spending Heatmap

A GitHub-style calendar showing your daily activity. Spot patterns and spending streaks at a glance.

Deep Analytics

Pie charts, bar graphs, and trend lines that break down spending by category, time period, and more.

Secure & Private

Your data stays on your device. No banks linked, no third-party access. 100% local storage.

Income vs Spend

Track income sources alongside expenses. See your real monthly surplus or deficit at all times.
